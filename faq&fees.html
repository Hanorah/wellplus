FAQ

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of healthcare services including primary care, specialized treatments, and wellness programs.

How can I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by filling out the form on our contact page or by calling our office directly.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact us to verify if we accept your specific insurance provider.

What are your business hours?

Our business hours are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

Is parking available at your facility?

Yes, we have free parking available for patients and visitors.

Can I bring a family member to my appointment?

Yes, you are welcome to bring a family member or caregiver to your appointment.

Do you offer telemedicine appointments?

Yes, we offer telemedicine appointments for certain healthcare services. Please inquire with our office for more information.

How can I refill my prescription?

You can request a prescription refill through our patient portal or by contacting our pharmacy directly.

Do you provide emergency medical services?

While we do not provide emergency medical services, we can refer you to nearby emergency facilities if needed.
