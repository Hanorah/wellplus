OUR PROCEDURES

How can we help you?

Aesthetic therapy leaves you with a glowing inner & outer radiance which has a powerful impact on both your personal appearance and overall presence, by creating a new sense of s elf-awareness leading to a positive expression of self-confidence.

We have created comprehensive programs of highly innovative skin & body treatments that integrate medical aesthetics with new, state-of-the-art techniques and procedures, programs jointly developed by Biolite’s doctors, skin therapists and leading international cosmetic laboratories.

Aesthetic therapy is part of the Biolite’s medical concept – a holistic approach that navigates health barriers considering a multi-faceted approach to assist the healing and rejuvenation process. A combination of state-of-the-art practices of international medical aesthetics, cosmetic surgery with exceptional wellness therapies. A detailed approach with attentiveness and empathy constitute as important elements of our practice.

Over the last two decades, Biolite has established a name as a leading wellness & aesthetic health care centre in Dubai.

Signature treatments at Biolite

Discover Biolite's most sought-after procedures, meticulously designed to enhance, rejuvenate, and celebrate your natural beauty.

DERMAL AND LIP FILLERS

Dermal fillers and lip fillers are cosmetic solutions meant to enhance your natural beauty and revitalize your appearance. Fillers restore volume, smooth deep creases, and enhance the shape, fullness, and contours of your lips.

SKIN BOOSTERS

Discover the rejuvenating power of skin boosters with Restylane, Juvederm, Profhilo & much more. (Achieve smoother, fuller lips, minimize wrinkles, and restore volume with Restylane’s proprietary XpresHAn Technology™.) Please look at skin boosters on our website for details.

MORPHEUS8

Morpheus8 combines radiofrequency (RF) energy with microneedling. Suitable for both face and body, Morpheus8 targets fine lines, wrinkles, sagging skin, stretch marks, and more. Ideal for all skin types, personalized for you. Biolite has achieved over 15,000 treatments.

SOFWAVE

Sofwave combines next-generation ultrasound technology with proprietary Synchronous Ultrasound Parallel Beam SUPERB™ technology to stimulate collagen production and improve skin laxity, fine lines, and wrinkles. Ideal for all skin types, Sofwave treatments are safe and effective with minimal downtime.

FOTONA 4D TREATMENT

Fotona 4D is a multi-dimensional laser procedure that addresses facial aging without surgery. This innovative technique works on deeper, medial and superficial connective structures of the skin while simultaneously targeting imperfections to give you a renewed, youthful appearance & skin tightening. It also targets eyes for dark circles & laxity & lips for natural plumping & brightening.

IPL PHOTO REJUVENATION

Illuminate your skin's true potential with IPL photo rejuvenation. This treatment uses intense pulsed light to target and diminish the appearance of age spots, sun damage, redness, rosacea & active acne, revealing a clearer and more radiant complexion.

ADVATX

Discover the benefits of Advalight laser treatments: FDA and CE-approved, they provide gentle, comfortable rejuvenation with no downtime. Address wrinkles, acne scars, dark spots, and more for dramatic skin improvement. Suitable for all skin types and ages.

SILK PEEL (DIAMOND GLOW BY ALLERGAN)

Get bright and soft skin with the Silk Peel Dermalinfusion. This medical-grade treatment not only exfoliates the top layers of the epidermis but also delivers topical nourishment tailored to individual concerns incorporating deep cleansing & manual extraction. Witness an immediate improvement in your skin’s brightness, hydration, and texture with a Silk Peel Dermalinfusion. The perfect skin prep before any intensive treatment.

PICO GENESIS SKIN REVITALIZATION

PICO Genesis offers a breakthrough in treating facial blemishes, pigmentation, melasma, and discoloration. This non-invasive laser treatment can correct discoloration and blemishing, delivering unique wavelengths at an ultra-fast rate. Suitable for all skin tones, PICO Genesis promotes a more uniform skin appearance.

THE CELL GYM

Cell Gym utilizes a specialized breathing mask for intermittent hypoxic training. Like altitude training, it enhances energy, weight management, metabolism, and athletic performance. Ideal for athletes, enthusiasts, professionals, and those battling fatigue.

SOMA SLEEP THERAPY

Soma Sleep Therapy utilizes binaural acoustic and vibrational techniques to induce deep relaxation and enhance sleep quality, reduce stress, boost creativity, and support anxiety treatment. Ideal for those with sleep issues, stress, anxiety, or focus challenges.

TRIPLE DETOX THERAPY

The Triple Detox iDome is a cutting-edge detox solution harnessing far infrared, plasma, and light technologies. Proven to eliminate carcinogenic waste and heavy metals at a deep cellular level, it revitalizes the body, aids muscle recovery, enhances mental clarity, and rejuvenates the skin. Ideal for inflammation, anxiety, chronic pain, and sleep issues.

OZONE THERAPY

Experience revitalization with Biolite Dubai's innovative 10 Pass Ozone therapy. This therapy harnesses ozone's unique properties to enhance health to manage a range of conditions, from fatigue to autoimmune diseases, circulatory issues, and skin health.
